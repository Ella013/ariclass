How to Create Your Ladder Game

Simple Steps to Create

  1. Enter a title for your worksheet
  2. Select the number of players (2–8)
  3. Type player names at the top and results at the bottom
  4. Click "Generate Ladder" for a new random ladder
  5. Print your worksheet using the print button

How to Play

  • Each player picks a column at the top
  • Follow your line straight down
  • When you hit a horizontal rung, move sideways
  • Continue down until you reach the bottom
  • Your result is where your line ends up!

Tips for Teachers

  • Use for random seat or group assignments
  • Assign homework or classroom duties randomly
  • Click "Show Answers" to reveal all paths
  • Generate a new ladder each time for variety
  • Works great as a fun classroom icebreaker
