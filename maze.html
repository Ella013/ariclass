How to Create Your Maze Puzzle

Simple Steps to Create

  1. Enter a title for your worksheet at the top
  2. Select the difficulty level (Easy 10x10, Medium 15x15, or Hard 20x20)
  3. Click "Generate Puzzle" to create a random maze
  4. Use "Show Answers" to display the solution path in red
  5. Print your worksheet using the print button

Educational Benefits

  • Improves problem-solving and critical thinking skills
  • Enhances spatial reasoning and visual perception
  • Develops patience and persistence
  • Perfect for brain training and cognitive development
  • Creates engaging activities for classroom or homework

Tips for Teachers

  • Start with Easy (10x10) for younger students
  • Use Medium (15x15) for most students
  • Challenge advanced students with Hard (20x20) mazes
  • Print with answer key (red path) for easy checking
  • Use as warm-up activities or problem-solving exercises
