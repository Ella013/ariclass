How to Use AriClass

AriClass makes it easy to create custom educational worksheets in seconds. Follow this guide to get started with our worksheet generator.

Getting Started

Creating worksheets with AriClass is simple and requires no registration. Just follow these basic steps:

  1. Choose a Worksheet Type: Select from Word Search, Unscramble, Crossword Puzzle, Matching Lists, or Bingo Game on our home page.
  2. Enter Your Content: Type or paste your words, sentences, or vocabulary into the input fields.
  3. Customize Settings: Adjust options like layout format, spacing, font size, and other preferences.
  4. Generate & Preview: Click the "Generate Worksheet" button to create your worksheet and preview it on the right side.
  5. Print: Use the print button to print your worksheet directly from the browser.

Worksheet Types

Word Search

Create engaging word search puzzles with custom vocabulary. Perfect for vocabulary practice and spelling reinforcement.

  • Enter up to 10 words in the input field
  • Choose uppercase or lowercase letters
  • Select grid size (8x8, 11x11, or 15x15)
  • Customize with options like diagonal words and reverse words

Unscramble

Generate sentence unscrambling exercises to help students learn word order and sentence structure.

  • Enter sentences, each ending with a period, question mark, or exclamation mark
  • Choose between 1 or 2 column layout
  • Adjust spacing between questions (Compact, Normal, or Wide)
  • Option to capitalize the first word in scrambled sentences

Crossword Puzzle

Create interactive crossword puzzles for vocabulary practice and concept review.

  • Enter words and their clues
  • Automatically generates crossword grid
  • Includes answer key for easy grading

Matching Lists

Create matching exercises to connect related words, concepts, or definitions.

  • Enter pairs of related items (words, definitions, translations, etc.)
  • Automatically shuffles and numbers the items
  • Includes "Show Answers" feature with numbered answers

Bingo Game

Generate fun bingo games with customizable board sizes for vocabulary practice and review activities.

  • Enter your vocabulary words
  • Choose board size (3x3, 4x4, or 5x5)
  • Select number of players (each gets a unique board)
  • Adjust font size (Small, Medium, or Large)
  • Customize worksheet title

Tips for Teachers

  • Prepare Your Content: Have your vocabulary words, sentences, or concepts ready before starting. This saves time and ensures accuracy.
  • Use Current Lessons: Create worksheets based on your current curriculum to reinforce learning and provide relevant practice.
  • Adjust Difficulty: Use different settings like grid size, spacing, and font size to match your students' skill levels.
  • Save for Later: Print multiple copies of the same worksheet for different classes or save the content to recreate later.
  • Combine Activities: Create different types of worksheets for the same vocabulary to provide varied practice opportunities.

Printing Your Worksheets

All worksheets are optimized for A4 paper size. When printing:

  • Click the "Print Worksheet" button in the preview area
  • In the print dialog, ensure "A4" is selected as paper size
  • Make sure margins are set to default (usually 20mm)
  • For multiple pages (like Bingo with multiple players), all pages will print automatically
  • Navigation and input sections are automatically hidden when printing

Common Questions
