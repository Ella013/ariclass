How to Use Writing Paper

1 Per Page

  • Full A4 page with English ruling lines
  • 3-line system: cap height, x-height (dashed), baseline
  • Includes Date and Name fields at the top

2 Per Page

  • Two sheets on one A4 page
  • Cut along the dashed line to separate
  • Great for shorter writing exercises

4 Per Page

  • Four mini sheets in a 2×2 grid
  • Cut along the cross lines to separate
  • Perfect for quick writing practice
