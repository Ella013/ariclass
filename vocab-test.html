How to Use Note Sheets

1 Per Page

  • Full A4 portrait page with writing lines
  • Includes Date and Name fields at the top
  • Great for longer vocabulary lists or note-taking

2 Per Page

  • Two sheets on one A4 page (landscape halves)
  • Cut along the dashed line to separate
  • Great for shorter vocabulary lists (saves paper!)

4 Per Page

  • Four mini sheets in a 2×2 grid on one A4 page
  • Cut out each cell separately
  • Perfect for quick quizzes or small word lists
